Good questions, honest answers

What users most want to know before they start.
How do the seven tools connect?

They build on each other: the readiness assessment produces your profile, the canvas describes the use case, scoring prioritizes, the governance check verifies, and the architecture generator builds the reference architecture from it — including ownership. Each result is automatically picked up in the next step. But you can also start with any single tool.

What can I use for free — and what do I need Pro for?

Five of the seven tools are usable for free (the readiness assessment as a compact quick scan) — including saving and the semantic context detection, which runs entirely without AI calls, plus a small daily AI allowance to try it out. Pro unlocks the architecture generator and the Compliance Center, plus PDF export, sharing, versions, and an expanded AI allowance. The full comparison — including what's not included — is laid out transparently on the pricing page.

Where is my data stored?

In the EU (Frankfurt), GDPR-compliant, with no cookies and no third-party tracking scripts. Your inputs belong to you — you can export or delete them at any time.

What does the AI analysis do with my inputs?

It supplements the rule-based results with suggestions — for example, matching components or a classification of your use case. Every AI suggestion is marked as such, and you decide with one click whether to accept it. Which model ran for what is shown transparently next to the result.

Do I need to use SAP for this to be worthwhile?

No. The tools are built vendor-independent. If SAP is part of your landscape, you'll additionally see matching best practices — if not, you simply won't see them.
